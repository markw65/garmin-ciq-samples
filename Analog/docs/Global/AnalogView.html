generateHandCoordinates(centerPoint as Lang.Array<Lang.Number>, angle as Lang.Float, handLength as Lang.Number, tailLength as Lang.Number, width as Lang.Number) as Lang.Array<Lang.Array<Lang.Float>>

This function is used to generate the coordinates of the 4 corners of the polygon used to draw a watch hand. The coordinates are generated with specified length, tail length, and width and rotated around the center point at the provided angle. 0 degrees is at the 12 o'clock position, and increases in the clockwise direction.

Parameters:

  • centerPoint —

    The center of the clock

  • angle —

    Angle of the hand in radians

  • handLength —

    The length of the hand from the center to point

  • tailLength —

    The length of the tail of the hand

  • width —

    The width of the watch hand

Returns:

  • The coordinates of the watch hand
